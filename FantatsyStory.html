  • What is the POV of the work?

    •   The POV for the work is the MC who has transmigrated into this ruthless world as an Extra.

  • What is the kind of character arc that the MC will go through in the entire story?

    •   At the starting the MC wants to gain power so that he can save himself from the horrors of the world.

      When asked the question, Who do you serve? His response would be Myself!.

      An arrogant response in a world where the fates of hunters are tied closely together.

      When asked the same question at the end, his response will still be Myself!

      But "Myself" here includes everyone around him and the people he is connected with.

      In the beginning the MC is a simple human.

      By the end the MC becomes a being that is spread across several creatures.

  • What is the question the work aims to raise and answer by the end?

      Where the normal people are constantly taken to face the reconining-

      What does it mean to hold the Key to salvation ? A key that can be used only if you sacrifice your sense of self.
