  • How does magic in this world work?

    •   Magic works differently for different races.

      For humans, magic is performed by inscribing runes onto their souls.

      The better they understand their rune and the more resonance there is between the rune and their soul, the better and stronger their magic.

      Each rune allows people the possibility to edit the various parameters of the world.

  • What are the parameters that Magic can interact with?

    •   Parameters are the basic runic compositions eeach and every item in the world can be represented with.

      For example, the position parameter of an object, which can be manipulated to teleport around the object by someone with a space rune.

      Someone with a vibration rune could also cause the position of atoms of the object to vibrate and make it extremely hot or cold by dampning vibration.

  • What are the distinct phases of Human magic Runic Manipulation Magic?

    •   Magic for humans is classified into seven distinct phases.

    1. Rune manifestation. : Rune manifestation involves simply passing mana to the rune and causing its basic effect to occur.

    2. Object Parameter Manipulation. : Various parameters of inanimate objects can be manipulated.

    3. Understanding Manipulation. : Rune effects can be controlled by implying personal understanding of the rune's effect.

    4. World Parameter Manipulation. : Parameters of the world can be modified, like time, space, temperature, humidity, etc.

    5. Soul Property Manipulation. : Runes can be used to modify the properties of souls based on personal understanding.

    6. Mana Property Manipulation. : Runes can be used to manipulate the properties of mana directly, making it harder for other people to control their own mana.

    7. Domain Expansion. : Runes can be used to create a domain where personal understanding of the person about their rune is the only way it works.

      Each phase of runic manipulation magic is deeply personal to each human and is based on their understanding of the rune engraved into their soul.

  • What are the distinct categories of Human magic Runic Manipulation Magic?

    •   Magic for humans is categoriesed into four different categories based on how unique the magic is to its user.

    1. Wand Magister : These magisters use wands made up of magical creature souls to manipulate mana easier by basing magic on their intent.

    2. Body Magister : These magisters use their bodies to directly manipulate mana and manifest spells based on their runes.

    3. Devotion Magister : These magisters pray to a deity to pass their intent along to the deity such that they can cast spells in the domain of their deity.

    4. Soul Magister (Deity) : These magisters have successfully created domains of their own magic where they can strictly implement the understanding of their runes.
